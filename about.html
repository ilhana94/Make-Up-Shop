The history of our brand

Makeup Shop was born out of a passion for beauty and the desire to give women of all ages access to top makeup products. We founded our brand in 2015 with one simple goal: to provide the latest and highest quality products, which will allow every woman to feel confident and beautiful in her own skin. The story of Makeup Shop began in a small studio, where two passionate enthusiasts, Ilhana and Delila, decided to combine their love for beauty and entrepreneurship. They started as a small online shop with a few selected products, and their goal was simple – to bring the best makeup products to the market, with personal attention to each customer. Over the years, Makeup Shop has grown, not only in its offer, but also in its business philosophy. We started to cooperate with global brands, but also to support local makeup artists and designers, in order to always offer products that are trendy and at the same time affordable for all women. Through our passion for innovation, we have created a community that shares the same love for make-up, beauty and confidence. Our philosophy has always been focused on quality, and that's why in the Makeup Shop you can find only carefully selected products that meet the highest standards. Whether you are a professional make-up artist, or someone who is just starting to explore the world of make-up, here you will find everything you need for a perfect look. As our shop grew, we continued to expand our range, offering everything from basic make-up products such as powder, mascara and blush, to luxury skin care products and exclusive limited editions. Our offer is not only rich - it is also personalized, because we want every woman to find the products that best suit her needs. Today, Makeup Shop is synonymous with trust and quality on the market, and our mission remains the same – to be there for every woman who wants to look and feel perfect.
